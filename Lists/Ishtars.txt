#In our analysis, we treat all Ištars, such as Ištar of Nineveh and Ištar of Arbail (Arbela), as independent entities from the deity designated only as “Ištar." In order to find the various Ištars with designations, we looked at the words after Ištar and in the following cases combined the words to one that can by automated methods be considered one word instead of several. We left out the prepositions and descriptive words, thus standardizing the various different way of lemmatising the same deity. The left word combination is the one found in the text and on the right after the arrows is the standardized combination.
