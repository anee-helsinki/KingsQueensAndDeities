#In our analysis, we treat different manifestations of Ištar, such as Ištar of Nineveh and Ištar of Arbela, as independent entities from the deity designated only as "Ištar." However, Oracc metadata does not differentiate between Ištars, and we had to develop a workflow to separate them. We discerned different Ištars on the basis of words occurring after the lemma "Ištar" in the dataset. The following list shows which lemma combinations were grouped together to designate different Ištars. We left out prepositions and certain descriptive words and thus created one standardized lemma for each Ištar. The lemma combination on the left is the one found in the text, followed by the standardized combination after the arrows.
